Day-wise Itinerary

Day 1-2 — Haridwar / Rishikesh

Arrive Haridwar, Ganga Aarti at Har Ki Pauri, drive to Rishikesh.

Day 3 — Yamunotri

Drive to Janki Chatti, trek to Yamunotri temple, puja.

Day 4-5 — Gangotri

Drive to Gangotri, Ganga puja, Gaumukh darshan.

Day 6-7 — Kedarnath

Drive to Gaurikund, trek/heli to Kedarnath, Shiva puja.

Day 8-9 — Badrinath

Drive to Badrinath, Vishnu puja, Mana village visit.

Day 10-11 — Return

Drive back to Haridwar/Rishikesh, departure.
