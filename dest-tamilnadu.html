About Tamil Nadu

Tamil Nadu is a land of ancient Dravidian culture, magnificent temples, classical music, and dance. The state is home to some of India's most spectacular temple architecture, including the famous Meenakshi Amman Temple in Madurai.

From the bustling city of Chennai to the serene hill station of Ooty, the beach town of Mahabalipuram, and the temple city of Thanjavur, Tamil Nadu offers a rich cultural experience unlike any other.

Best Time to Visit

November to February is the best time. Avoid May-June due to extreme heat. The northeast monsoon (Oct-Dec) brings rain to Chennai.
