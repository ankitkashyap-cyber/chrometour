Day-wise Itinerary

Day 1 — Shimla Arrival

Arrive Shimla, Mall Road walk, Christ Church visit.

Day 2 — Shimla Sightseeing

Jakhu Temple, Kufri, Indian Institute of Advanced Study.

Day 3 — Shimla to Manali

Scenic drive to Manali via Kullu Valley, river rafting.

Day 4 — Rohtang Pass

Rohtang Pass excursion, snow activities, Solang Valley.

Day 5 — Manali to Kasol

Drive to Kasol, Parvati Valley trek, camp setup.

Day 6 — Kasol Trek

Kheerganga trek, hot springs, stargazing at night.

Day 7 — Departure

Drive back to Delhi/Chandigarh, departure.
