About Uttarakhand

Uttarakhand, known as "Dev Bhoomi" (Land of Gods), is home to some of India's most sacred pilgrimage sites including the Char Dham — Yamunotri, Gangotri, Kedarnath, and Badrinath.

Beyond spirituality, Uttarakhand offers stunning Himalayan landscapes, adventure sports in Rishikesh, wildlife in Jim Corbett, and the serene hill stations of Mussoorie and Nainital.

Best Time to Visit

May-June and September-October for Char Dham. December-February for snow. Rishikesh is good year-round.
