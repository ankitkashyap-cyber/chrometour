About Himachal Pradesh

Himachal Pradesh is a paradise for adventure lovers and nature enthusiasts. With snow-capped peaks, dense forests, apple orchards, and crystal-clear rivers, it's one of India's most beautiful states.

From the colonial charm of Shimla to the adventure hub of Manali and the hippie haven of Kasol, Himachal offers diverse experiences. Trekking, river rafting, paragliding, and skiing are popular activities.

Best Time to Visit

March to June for pleasant weather. December to February for snow. Avoid July-August (heavy monsoon in some areas).
