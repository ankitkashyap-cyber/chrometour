About Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is one of the world's most iconic monuments, a UNESCO World Heritage Site located in Agra, Uttar Pradesh. Built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal, this white marble masterpiece is a symbol of eternal love.

The best time to visit is at sunrise when the marble glows golden pink. The complex includes the main mausoleum, mosque, guest house, and beautiful Mughal gardens.

Best Time to Visit

October to March is ideal. Avoid summer months (April-June) due to extreme heat. Monsoon (July-September) offers lush greenery but humidity is high.
