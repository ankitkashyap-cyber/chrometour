Day-wise Itinerary

Day 1 — Jaipur Arrival

Arrive Jaipur, hotel check-in, evening Chokhi Dhani folk show.

Day 2 — Jaipur Sightseeing

Amber Fort, City Palace, Hawa Mahal, Jantar Mantar.

Day 3 — Jaipur to Jodhpur

Drive to Jodhpur, Mehrangarh Fort, Blue City walk.

Day 4 — Jodhpur to Jaisalmer

Drive to Jaisalmer, Golden Fort, camel safari at sunset.

Day 5 — Jaisalmer to Udaipur

Drive to Udaipur, City Palace, Saheliyon ki Bari.

Day 6 — Udaipur

Lake Pichola boat ride, Jagdish Temple, Fateh Sagar Lake.

Day 7 — Udaipur Free Day

Shopping, local market, evening cultural show.

Day 8 — Departure

Airport/station drop, departure.
